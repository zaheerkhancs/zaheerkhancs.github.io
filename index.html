Biography

Zaheer received the bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from the University of Peshawar, and the master’s degree in Computer Science from Abdul Wali Khan University Mardan, Pakistan, in 2016. He completed his PhD degree in Computer Science and Technology from the School of Computer Science and Information Technology, Nanjing University of Aeroanutics and Astronuatics, Nanjing, China. He is a member of the Pattern Recognition and Bioinformatics Group. His current research interests include machine learning and deep learning in bioinformatics and image processing.
